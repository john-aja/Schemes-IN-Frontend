Types of
schemes

Schemes are launched for the purpose of improving the livelihood of the people and providing security for leading a better life.

Each scheme is launched to provide benefits to the individual in certain areas of their life.

Schemes have been basically of three-types depending upon funding & implementing agencies.

  • 01 Centrally Sponsored Schemes
  • 02 Central Sector Schemes
  • 03 State Government Schemes

*Note: As of now schemes has been only implemented for Tamilnadu state government
